Why Water Matters

Water is not just a resource — it is the bloodstream of our planet. Yet, pollution, overuse, and climate change are pushing water systems to their limits.

Our Mission: Keep Water Alive

Blue Pulse Network is a community-powered movement focused solely on protecting the planet’s most fragile and essential element: water.

From restoring urban rivers and defending wetlands to fighting plastic in oceans and promoting smarter water use in cities, we synchronize local action with global impact.

Founded in 2012, we now connect over 60,000 volunteers, scientists, and storytellers across 135 watersheds worldwide.
